# Grow With Neetu Design System

## Pattern
Trust & Authority + Hero-Centric education portal.

Primary conversion goals:
- Watch latest YouTube lectures.
- Subscribe to the YouTube channel.
- Explore structured subject and exam hubs.

## Visual Style
Accessible & Ethical education UI with Soft UI Evolution and Bento Grid accents.

Use clean white surfaces, light blue backgrounds, restrained depth, rounded cards, clear section hierarchy and high-contrast typography.

## Colors
- Background: `#f8fbff`
- Surface: `#ffffff`
- Text: `#102033`
- Muted text: `#5b6b80`
- Primary blue: `#0b5fff`
- Primary green: `#00a676`
- Soft blue: `#eaf2ff`
- Soft green: `#e9fbf4`
- Border: `#dbe7f5`

## Typography
Use Inter via `next/font`. Keep headings bold and compact, with generous line height for body content.

## Interaction Rules
- All clickable elements need visible hover and focus states.
- Use 150-300ms transitions.
- Respect reduced-motion preferences by avoiding required animation.
- CTAs should be repeated on long pages, especially video, subject and exam pages.

## Accessibility Checklist
- Maintain WCAG AA text contrast.
- Do not use emojis as icons; use Lucide SVG icons.
- Ensure keyboard navigation works across links, buttons and search.
- Use semantic headings and landmarks.
- Keep tap targets at least 44px tall where practical.

## Anti-Patterns
- Avoid neon palettes, dark-only experiences, heavy gradients, decorative clutter and autoplaying media.
- Avoid generic personal-brand pages where subject and exam intent should lead.
